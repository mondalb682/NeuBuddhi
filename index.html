About Us

Welcome to Neu Buddhi! We provide fun and engaging communication development materials for children. Our resources are designed to support learning in areas such as emotions, language concepts, and more, making education enjoyable for young learners.

About the Founder

Neu Buddhi is owned by Ambika Bose, a dedicated Audiologist and Speech-Language Pathologist. She holds a BASLP & MASLP from AYJNISHD (D), West Bengal University of Health Sciences.

With her expertise, Ambika is passionate about creating high-quality educational materials that cater to the communication development needs of children.
